    This is RENCI's Geoanalytics cyberinfrastructure portal.

    Geoanalytics is an Open Source spatial data and analytics infrastructure that brings together many of the open source Python geography projects into a CMS and collaboration framework. Geoanalytics handles both classical and exotic formats in a unified way, using a system of drivers that connect these data sources to standard OGC web services.

    Spatial data can be catalogued, described, queried, and Geoanalytics' CMS can serve as a host to a myriad of domain-specific applications.

    Geoanalytics was developed at RENCI to deal with various projects centered around providing application infrastructure around scientific geographic data. It has been used in myriad projects at RENCI in:

    • Environmental and Earth sciences (hydrology, weather modeling)
    • Epidemiology and Public Health
    • Decision support
    • Emergency management and mitigation
    • Digital humanities
